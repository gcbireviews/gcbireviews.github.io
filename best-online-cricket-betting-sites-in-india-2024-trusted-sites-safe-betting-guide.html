Most Popular Cricket Bets

Cricket betting in India offers a vast array of options, but some bets consistently top the charts for their popularity among Indian fans. Here are some of the most favored cricket wagers:

  1. Match Winner: This is the most straightforward and popular bet, where you simply predict the team that will win the match.
  2. Top Batsman/Bowler: This bet involves predicting the player who will score the most runs (batsman) or take the most wickets (bowler) in the match.
  3. Total Runs/Wickets: You can bet on whether the total number of runs scored or wickets taken in the match will be over or under a certain value set by the cricket betting site.
  4. In-Play Betting: This exciting option allows you to wager on various aspects of the match as it unfolds, such as the next batsman to be dismissed, the next six runs to be scored, or the team to win the next over.
  5. Series Winner: Predict the team that will win an entire tournament or series.
  6. Margin of Victory: This bet involves predicting the winning team and the margin of their victory, such as by a specific number of runs or wickets.
  7. Individual Player Performance: Beyond top batsman/bowler, you can bet on various aspects of individual player performances, such as whether a specific player will score a fifty or a century, or take a certain number of wickets.
  8. Prop Bets: These are less common but can add excitement, allowing you to bet on unique events within the match, such as the coin toss outcome or the team batting first to score a specific number of runs in the powerplay overs.
