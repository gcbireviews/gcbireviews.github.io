Common Types of Cricket Bets You NEED to Know to Win Big in India (Don’t Get Fooled!)

April 3, 2024April 3, 2024Abhishek Rout

Unleash your inner cricket pundit with a comprehensive guide to types of cricket bets! Whether you’re a seasoned fan or a curious newcomer, this article equips you with the knowledge to navigate the exciting world of cricket betting in India.

 

Delve into various bet types, from the classic match winner to in-play thrills and strategic future bets. Discover how to choose the right bet for you and maximize your cricket viewing experience.

 

So, grab your lucky charm, and get ready to dive deep into the diverse world of types of cricket bets!

The Significance of Understanding Cricket Bet Types

The Indian cricket betting market thrives on the thrill of prediction. By familiarizing yourself with the different cricket bet types, you gain a significant advantage. Here’s why understanding these options is vital:

  • Making Informed Choices: With a vast array of bets available, knowing the mechanics of each option allows you to choose bets that align with your knowledge and risk tolerance.

  • Identifying Value Bets: Not all odds are created equal. Understanding bet types empowers you to recognize situations where the bookmaker’s odds might be skewed, offering a potentially profitable opportunity (value bet).

  • Enhancing Cricket Viewing: Exploring different bet types adds another layer of excitement to watching a cricket match. You become more invested in specific aspects of the game, following not just the overall outcome but also individual player performances or key milestones.

Unveiling the Diverse Cricket Bet Types in India

Indian bookmakers offer a plethora of cricket bet types, categorized into various sections. Let’s explore these categories in detail:

Match Result Bets

These bets focus on the ultimate outcome of the match. Here are the main options:

  • Match Winner (Outright Winner, Moneyline):¬† This is the most basic and popular bet.¬† You merely forecast which team will come out on top.

  • Draw (Tie Betting):¬† In rare instances, a cricket match might end in a tie. This bet allows you to wager on this specific outcome.

Team Performance Bets

These bets delve deeper into the performance of specific teams within a match. Some popular options include:

  • Top Team Batsman/Bowler (Player Performance Betting): You predict the player from a particular team who will score the most runs (batsman) or take the most wickets (bowler) throughout the match.

  • Team of the Top Batsman/Bowler (Team Props): Here, you wager on the team that will have the player with the highest runs scored (batsman) or most wickets taken (bowler) in the entire match.

  • Most Sixes/Fours by a Team (Team Batting Props):¬† This bet allows you to predict which team will hit the most sixes or fours throughout the innings.

Individual Player Performance Bets

These bets focus on the performance of specific players in a match. Some examples include:

  • Top Batsman/Bowler (Across the Entire Match): Similar to the team version, you predict the player with the highest runs scored (batsman) or most wickets taken (bowler) across the entire match, regardless of team affiliation.

  • Player of the Match (Specific to Test Matches):¬† This bet is exclusive to Test matches, where you predict the player adjudged as the “Player of the Match” by the official panel.

  • Most Wickets by a Bowler (In a Specific Innings): You wager on the bowler who will take the most wickets in a particular innings (first or second).

  • Most Runs Scored by a Batsman (In a Specific Innings): Here, you predict the batsman who will score the highest number of runs in a specific innings.

Innings-Based Bets

These bets center around specific aspects of an inning within a match. Here are some popular options:

  • Innings Runs (Over/Under) (Totals Betting): The bookmaker sets a total number of runs expected to be scored in an inning. You can then wager on whether the actual number of runs scored will be over or under this predetermined value.

  • Fall of Wickets (Over/Under): Similar to runs, a total number of wickets expected to fall in an inning is set. You can bet on whether the actual number of wickets will be over or under this number.

  • Highest Opening Partnership: This bet focuses on the opening batsmen. You predict the pair that will score the highest number of runs in their combined opening partnership.

Other Bets

Beyond the core categories, bookmakers offer additional cricket bet types to add variety. Some examples include:

  • Toss Winner (Coin Toss Prediction):¬† A simple bet on which team will win the coin toss at the beginning of the match.

  • Most Run Outs: Predict the team that will affect the most run-outs in the match.

  • Method of Dismissal (Specific Player):¬† This is a more specific bet where you predict the way a particular player will be dismissed (caught out, bowled, etc.).

In-Play Betting Options

In-play betting, alternatively referred to as live betting, enables you to place bets on a cricket match while it is happening. This adds a whole new dimension of excitement to watching the game.

 

With in-play betting, the odds constantly fluctuate based on the live action. You can react to the changing dynamics of the match and place bets on various aspects like:

 

  • Next dismissal method (caught out, bowled, etc.)

  • Runs scored off the next ball

  • Whether a specific batsman will score a boundary in the next over

  • Whether a particular bowler will take a wicket in his next over

Tips for In-Play Betting

  • Stay Focused: The fast-paced nature of in-play betting demands sharp focus and quick decision-making.

  • React to the Situation: Analyze the live action and identify betting opportunities based on what’s happening on the field.

  • Handle Your Bankroll: Establish a budget for in-play betting and adhere to it consistently. The dynamic nature can be tempting, so responsible management is crucial.

Future Bets and Outrights

Future bets or outrights involve placing wagers on events that will occur throughout a tournament or season, rather than a single match.¬† Here are some popular options:

 

  • Tournament Winner Bets:¬† Predict the team that will emerge victorious in a major cricket tournament like the IPL or World Cup.

  • Season-Long Bets:¬† These bets focus on individual player performances throughout a tournament or season. Examples include:

  • Top run-scorer in the IPL

  • Most wickets taken in the World Cup

Factors Influencing Future Bets

When placing future bets, consider these factors:

  • Team Form: Analyze recent performances of teams and players to assess their current strengths and weaknesses.

  • Player Injuries: Key player injuries can significantly impact a team’s performance, so stay updated on team news.

  • Tournament History: Consider past performance of teams and players in specific tournaments to gain insights.

Exotic Bets and Prop Bets

Exotic bets and prop bets are less common but can offer potentially high rewards for those willing to take a calculated risk. Here’s a closer look:

  • Boundary Bets:¬† These bets focus on the number of boundaries (fours and sixes) hit in a match or innings. You can predict whether the total number will be over or under a set value.

  • Over/Under Bets: As mentioned earlier, over/under bets are prevalent across various categories.¬† Here, you predict whether a specific event (e.g., total runs scored by a team in an innings, number of sixes hit by a batsman) will be over or under a predetermined number set by the bookmaker.

Tips for Exotic Bets

These bets can be riskier, so approach them cautiously. Conduct thorough research, analyze player statistics, and consider the pitch conditions before placing a bet.

 

Choosing the Right Bet for You

With the vast array of cricket bet types available, selecting the right one can be overwhelming. Below are several crucial elements to contemplate when deciding:

Match Format (Test, ODI, T20)

Different cricket formats have distinct playing styles and scoring rates.¬† Understanding the format is crucial for choosing suitable bets.¬† For example, “top batsman” bets might be more suited for T20s due to the emphasis on quick scoring.

Pitch Conditions (Flat, Turning, Seaming)

The pitch significantly impacts the game’s flow. Flat pitches favor batsmen, while turning pitches are bowler-friendly.¬† Analyze pitch reports to understand how it might influence player performance and choose bets accordingly.

Team Composition and Strengths/Weaknesses

Analyze team lineups and identify their strengths and weaknesses.¬† For instance, a team with a strong bowling attack might be a good choice for “most wickets” bets.

Player Form and Recent Performances

Research player form and recent performances to understand their current condition.¬† A batsman in good form might be a suitable choice for “top batsman” bets.

 

By considering these factors, you can make informed decisions and choose cricket bet types that align with your knowledge, risk tolerance, and overall betting strategy.

People Also Ask (PAA) Section

What are the most popular types of cricket bets in India?

They are Match Winner, Top Batsman/Bowler, Innings Runs (Over/Under), Toss Winner.

 

How do I place an in-play bet on cricket matches?

Choose a reputable bookmaker, fund your account, access the in-play section, browse available bets, place your bet, and follow the action.

Unique bets in Indian cricket betting might include individual batsman milestones (50/100 runs) or special bets on prominent Indian players.

 

What factors should I consider when making player performance bets?

Player form, matchup against opposition, playing conditions, and batting/bowling position.

Final Thought

In essence, grasping the intricacies of cricket bet types opens up a world of possibilities for bettors in India. By understanding the nuances of each bet type and leveraging strategic insights, enthusiasts can elevate their betting experience while navigating the vibrant landscape of Indian cricket betting.
